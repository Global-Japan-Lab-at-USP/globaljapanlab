Global Japan Lab

This initiative aims to introduce Global Japan Studies at the University of Sao Paulo (USP) by fostering interdisciplinary methodologies, multilingual pedagogy, and sustained engagement in international research networks.

About This Project

This website serves as a static prototype for the FAPESP International Thematic Grant application by Dr. Seth Jacobowitz (PI) and team. This project proposes targeted investments in academic resources, student and faculty research support, and digital infrastructure for establishing a cutting-edge Global Japan Studies program at USP.
